1. Sketch diagrammatically the steps for sampling and reconstruction of a continuous signal. Explain it!

original continuous signal -> antialiasing filter -> sampling -> reconstruction filter -> reconstructed signal

We use an antialiasing filter to eliminate aliasing (overlapping peaks in the frequency domain). Then we sample which creates
infinite copies of the Fourier transfrom of the signal (because of the Dirac comb) and we need to filter that with a reconstruction filter.
We reconstruct the signal which won't be the original signal but the signal that has been filtered by the antialiasing filter.
Both the antialiasing and reconstruction filters are low pass filters.
